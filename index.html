💪 Weekly Strength Training Plan

🏋️ Monday – Full Body Strength

🏋️‍♂️Goblet Squat: Hold a dumbbell at chest height, feet shoulder-width apart. Squat down by bending knees and hips, keeping chest upright. Return to standing.

🏋️Bent-Over Row: With a slight bend in your knees and a hinge at your hips, pull dumbbells or barbell to your ribcage, squeezing shoulder blades.
Watch Demo
🛋️Dumbbell Chest Press: Lie on a bench holding dumbbells at chest level. Press them upwards until arms extend, then lower back slowly.
Watch Demo
🏋️‍♀️Romanian Deadlift: With a slight knee bend, hinge at the hips to lower dumbbells along your thighs. Keep back straight and return to standing.

🧘Plank: On forearms and toes, hold a straight line from head to heels. Engage core and avoid sagging.
